Our Roots

Born in 2023 in Mungeli, Ibexx Organics Private Limited is a proud indigenous brand built on the principles of Quality, Precision, and Regional pride. We didn't just start a company; we started a mission to bring world class beverage standards to the heart of Chhattisgarh.

As a Vocal for Local initiative, Ibexx is dedicated to proving that a home-grown brand from our state can compete with the best in the world.

Premium Hydration, Locally Crafted

Our journey begins with Packaged Drinking Water that sets a new benchmark for purity. Processed in our state of the art facility (FSSAI Lic. No. 10526025000016) every bottle of Ibexx reflects our commitment to safety and excellence. We believe that premium quality shouldn't be an import it should be Indigenous.

Our Vision

To become Chhattisgarh's most trusted and Iconic beverage brand, recognized for elevating local craftsmanship to global premium standards. We envision a future where Ibexx is the heartbeat of every celebration from pure hydration to innovative refreshments while leading the way in environmental stewardship for our home state.


The Spirit of Chhattisgarh. Premium packaged drinking water and refreshing beverages crafted with excellence and regional pride.
